Dietary Info

Gluten-free options are available. All meals are nut-free.

About us

The Hive Free Breakfast & Lunch provides an essential service to students, faculty, and staff at the Loyola campus, which is a food desert with very few dining or grocery options. The HFBL also provides a unique oppurtunity to foster a sense of community around food, and everyone works hard in creating a safer space to share and learn cooking skills together. The HFBL aims to provide quality, nutritious food while considering the social and environmental impacts of the food we consume.

Allergies

The Hive Free Breakfast & Lunch is not an allergen-free kitchen. While we try to keep a close eye on potential allergens, when we state that a particular meal is wheat-, peanut-, nut-, or dairy-free, it means we did not actively use those ingredients, and we tried to keep these ingredients as far away as possible from the meal.

If you have an allergy please speak with the cooks or volunteers that helped prepare the meal.

Location

Located in the Hive Cafe Loyola, on the second floor of the SC building next to the library | SC200 building, at 7141 Sherbrooke West.

We are also please to offer: a community fridge, a free grocery table, and open access to our beautiful space during the day as a study or chill space.

The Hive Free Breakfast & Lunch space and kithen can be rented for events of any kind and size.
